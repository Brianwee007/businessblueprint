We need to calculate the approximate cost per horse per month for every horse in your barn. There are expenses that every horse incurs — hay, grain, shavings, and bedding — regardless of who owns them.

🩺Additional Horse Cost Per Month — Horses You OwnFarrier, vet, dental & more
📋 How to calculate this
Beyond the shared expenses above, horses you own have additional costs — farrier visits, vet care, dental, and any specialty supplements. These don't apply to boarders (that's their owner's expense).

Review your last 3 months of vet, farrier, and dental bills for the horses you own, enter each category below, then enter how many horses you own. We'll calculate your approximate additional cost per owned horse per month. This number — added to the cost per horse above — gives you your total cost per owned horse per month.
